The Best Movies According to Fagun

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

The White Tiger

I like the satirical themes in the movie along with a touch of reality. It accurately depicts the divide between society.

Grave Of The Fireflies

It is an anime set back in time for Japan and exploits your emotions if you connect with the characters. It is not a fun or a happy anime film but definately worth your time if you like to learn about the nuances of life.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This is a proper bollywood movie which includes light fun, love, sadness, satirical poetry and poetic justice. It is about a group of guy friends reuniting after a long time to go on an international trip, where they encounter the differences amongst each other. And end up realising why they became friends in the first place after a little drama and learning from each other's lives.
